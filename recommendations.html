Recommendations

Letters of recommendation from professors and colleagues

My name is Mihai Boicu, and I am an Associate Professor at George Mason University, with expertise in Artificial Intelligence, System Prototyping, and Engineering Education. I had the pleasure to mentor Ms. Garimella Sri Vidya starting in FALL 2023 when she joined my research group and was involved in various projects and activities. Together with Dr. Ioulia Rytikova, I also had the pleasure to guide Ms. Vidya in an engineering education project titled: "Anti-racism, Inclusion, Diversity and Equity in Database Curriculum Through Group Research Projects on Historical, Social and Ethical Database Related Topics Curriculum Grant."

Ms. Vidya has a rich contribution in the research group performing a diverse range of research related activities: literature review, curriculum development, test question design and validation, educational experiment design, data analysis and interpretation. Some highlights of her activity are the design and implementation of a pool of questions to randomly check reading comprehension for required reading materials and collection, analysis and presentation of survey data.

One aspect that I observed for Ms. Vidya is her extensive knowledge and skills in data analysis that is doubled by her eagerness to learn new things and expand her expertise. She is meticulous in data collection, detailed in data analysis and synthetic in data presentation, showing proficiency in Python programming, data pre-processing, and matplotlib. She worked diligently to create intuitive graphs to explain the results of analyzing complex data sets.

Also, Ms. Vidya showed dependable soft collaboration skills as she worked all the time in a research team and continuously communicated and collaborated with other team members.

Dr. Mihai Boicu

Associate Professor & Co-Director

George Mason University
